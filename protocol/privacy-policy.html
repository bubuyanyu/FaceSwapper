AI Photo Generation Feature

By using the FusionFaces AILab, including the AI filter feature, AI templates feature, or text to image/headshot feature, We will analyze the photos you uploaded and find out the facial key points (like eyes, nose, and mouth points) of the face in the photos for face processing purposes. PLEASE NOTE THAT WE DO NOT collect any face data. After the analyzing process ends all data will be deleted within 7 days, no data will be saved permanently.

Specially, in order to provide an AI Generation feature, the complete AI generate process is:

  1. You input the prompt content about the style you wish to generate;
  2. You agree to transfer a photo to our server；
  3. Our server detects a face in your photo;
  4. Find required facial key points (the set of points varies with the effect);
  5. Depends on your prompt content and apply the effect to your photo based on the detected key points.

PLEASE NOTE THAT AFTER THE GENERATE PROCESS IS COMPLETED, THE PHOTOS YOU UPLOADED WILL BE AUTOMATICALLY DELETED WITHIN 7 DAYS.
